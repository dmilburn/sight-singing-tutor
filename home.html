Welcome to the piano sight reading page by Sam Post, level 1-2

First, click level 1 or level 2 to generate two new exercises.
Click the next button to start the first one. There will be 2 second delay, then the blackout will start scrolling across the screen, so be ready! You can alter the speed by entering a number 1-10 before you start. 1 if very slow, 10 is very, very fast. Click the last button when you're ready for the second example. You can click for new examples at any time without refreshing the page.

The sight reading page is in the alpha stage of development. Email samueljpost@gmail.com with questions or bug reports.
